About the Team

Orange Chaos has competed in FIRST Robotics Competition since 2012, bringing together students passionate about engineering, software, and design. We're a team that builds from the ground up — mechanical, electrical, and software all in-house.

Our scouting platform, ScoutingPASS, is built and maintained by the team itself — designed to give us a competitive edge in alliance selection and match strategy.
